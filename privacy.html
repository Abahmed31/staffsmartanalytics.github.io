Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Overview

StaffSmart Analytics (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy and data. We collect only the minimum information required to respond to your inquiries and deliver workforce analytics services.

Data We Collect

  • Business contact information voluntarily submitted through our forms
  • Operational, non-PII workforce data used for analysis
  • Website analytics for performance and usability tracking

No PII Acceptance

We expressly prohibit uploading personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Names, SSNs, addresses, DOBs
  • Medical or clinical data
  • Employee personnel files

How We Use Data

  • To respond to inquiries
  • To provide workforce analytics reports
  • To improve our services and website

Data Retention

We retain only data needed to complete analysis and delete datasets upon request.

Security Controls

  • Encrypted transfer via HTTPS
  • Restricted analyst access
  • Secure deletion processes

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at info@staffsmartanalytics.com.
